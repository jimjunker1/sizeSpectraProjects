Appendix J. The prediction of power-law exponents in the generalized MTE model.

The mathematical form of the prediction on the power-law exponent of ISD, - (λ+b1), is very similar with the original metabolic theory. Here, we estimated the b1 from the linear fitting of size-TL relationship, TL = b0 + b1 logM. The empirical power-law exponents were estimated from K-means grouped ISD (Fig. H1E, F, G, H, L, M, and N). We specified λ as 0.75 as predicted by the MTE (Brown et al 2004) or λ as 1.06 as predicted by the recent model for animals living in a 3-D environment (Pawar et al. 2012). We found that the estimated b1’s are closer to the empirical observations if we specified λ as 1.06. However, as we explained in the main text, the value of λ has no effect on our conclusion on the secondary structure of ISD.
