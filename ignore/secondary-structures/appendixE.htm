Appendix E. The distribution of different K-means groups in time.

Fig. E1. The data were lumped into seven groups by K-means clustering according to the similarity of their isotope signature (a proxy for trophic structure). The distribution of these groups in time roughly followed a seasonal pattern. Different colors represent different K-means groups. The disturbance events occurring in the reservoir, including typhoons open triangles) and overturns (solid triangles), were also labeled above the panel.
